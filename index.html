HiFi Analyzer

Privacy Policy

Effective: February 28, 2026

HiFi Analyzer is developed and published by the developer of the HiFi series applications. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles user information.

01 Information We Collect

HiFi Analyzer does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

The App:

  • Does not require user registration
  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not collect device identifiers
  • Does not track user activity
  • Does not use analytics services
  • Does not display advertisements
02 Audio Files & Storage Access

The App may request access to local storage in order to:

  • Open audio files selected by the user
  • Analyze audio data locally on the device
All audio processing is performed entirely on the user's device.

The App does not:

  • Upload audio files
  • Store audio files on external servers
  • Share audio content with third parties
03 Internet Access

HiFi Analyzer does not require an internet connection for core functionality.

If internet permission exists in future versions, it will not be used to collect or transmit personal information without updating this Privacy Policy.
04 Third-Party Services

HiFi Analyzer does not integrate with:

  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics platforms
  • Cloud storage services
  • Social media services
05 Data Security

Since the App does not collect or transmit data, no user information is stored on external servers.

All file operations occur locally on the device.

06 Children's Privacy

HiFi Analyzer does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

07 Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
